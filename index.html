How It Works

From first contact to structured loan file

MyLOA's AI handles the entire intake and coordination journey — compliantly, automatically, across every channel.

01

Borrower reaches out

Via text, voice, email, or app — MyLOA replies in seconds with a warm, AI-disclosed introduction.

02

Intake runs in the background

Conversational AI guides each borrower through a complete loan application without rigid forms, capturing every detail with verification at each step.

03

Compliance is automatic

Federal and state rules are enforced before any data is processed, recorded, or transmitted.

04

Loan officer receives the file

A clean, organized application — verified, structured, and ready to act on — lands in the licensed loan officer's queue. The AI never underwrites.

Compliance First

The AI that knows its lane

MyLOA is architected from the ground up around regulatory compliance. The AI handles intake and coordination. Licensed loan officers handle underwriting. Always.

  • Federal mortgage law and state privacy law enforced automatically across every interaction
  • Sensitive borrower data handled with regulated retention and disposal protocols
  • Recording and AI-disclosure requirements satisfied at the start of every conversation
  • Audit-ready documentation captured for every action, every channel
