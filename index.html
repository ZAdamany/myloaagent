How It Works

From first contact to structured loan file

The Centaur AI Engine handles the entire intake journey — compliantly, automatically, across every channel.

01

Borrower Makes Contact

Via SMS, voice call, email, or mobile app — MyLOA meets borrowers where they are with instant, intelligent response.

02

AI Intake Engine Activates

The Centaur AI Engine guides borrowers through a structured intake flow, extracting and validating all required loan data.

03

Compliance Gates Run

TCPA consent, RESPA disclosures, and community property protocols are enforced automatically before any data is processed.

04

Loan Officer Receives File

A clean, structured loan file — verified, organized, and ready for review — lands in the licensed LO's queue. The AI never underwrites.

Compliance First

The AI that knows its lane

MyLOA is architected from the ground up around regulatory compliance. The Centaur AI Engine handles intake — licensed loan officers handle underwriting. Always.

  • TCPA safe harbor with documented prior express written consent
  • RESPA § 8 filtering — AI never provides referral-adjacent advice
  • Community property protocol for all applicable states
  • SSN ephemeral storage — never persisted by the AI layer

  • DECLINED state hard-stop — no re-engagement without explicit consent
